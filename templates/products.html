Duckies

Choose from all different kinds of duckies. We sell duckies in bulk, whether by the box full, by the dozen, by the pallet which includes 20 boxes, and by the truck load which includes 20 pallets (that's a lot of ducks)!

Box of Duckies

Individual boxes of duckies. If you only need a few at a time, this is what you're looking for! Individual boxes are only sold in yellow color. Please make sure that this is what you want prior to ordering.

Dozen of Duckies

Exactly 12 boxes of duckies. No more, no less. For those that need a lot of duckies but not too many. Discounts are available if ordering a dozen over individual boxes. Different color options are also available.

Pallet of Duckies

Need a few more than just a few boxes or a dozen? Order a pallet of duckies! One pallet contains 20 boxes of duckies. Different color options are also available.

Truck Load of Duckies

If you are in serious need of some duckies, don't worry! Rubber Ducky Inc has you covered with our biggest order we have, a TRUCKLOAD! Different color options are also available. However, we cannot mix and match ducky colors with this type of order.
